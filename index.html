The counter uses midpoint estimates where ranges exist. Land animal data from FAOSTAT is precise. Aquatic data carries moderate uncertainty. The insect pesticide figure (35 quadrillion) dominates the total and carries the most uncertainty, but represents the best available estimate of scale.

The counter ticks at ~1,110,534,258 per second. That is 84.35B land animals + 2.4T aquatic + ~35 quadrillion insects = ~35,021,808,350,000,000 total per year, divided by 31,536,000 seconds in a year.
